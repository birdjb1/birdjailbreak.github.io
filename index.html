How to Jailbreak a Bird

  1. Poke a T8 screwdriver through the big QR code sticker on the Bird to unscrew the 6 faceplate screws. Pull the faceplate off, then unscrew the circuit board underneath.
  3. Turn the circuit board around and unplug it from the Bird. Pop out the SIM card just to be safe.
  4. Connect a new Xiaomi M365 compatible board. I sell these in the eBay store.
  5. Ride into the sunset.
